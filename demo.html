Reflection.js

Reflects page content to a header or a footer

Simply wrap your content in div.reflective and add a div.reflection-container outside of the div.reflective wrapper.

Works with horizontal and vertical scrolling.

Defaults to reflecting on the bottom. Add the class "top" to your div.reflection-container to move the reflection to the top.
